Things to do in New Zealand

There are lots of fun activities wherever you go, and whatever your tastes include.

  • Hike the Milford Trail
  • Kayak, sail, parasail, dive and snorkel
  • Swim with dolphins or watch whales
  • Sample wine at local vineyards
  • Relax in geothermal mud baths and hot springs
  • Nerd out at Hobbitron, the set used in Lord of the Rings and The Hobbit

There are lots of geothermal pools near Rotorua on the North Island. The Shire set used in The Hobbit is a popular destination near Matamata. Bay of Islands offers great snorkeling and kayaking.
